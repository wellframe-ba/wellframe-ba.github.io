What the AI actually does

Three agents. Each replaces 4-8 hours of manual work per week.

Production

Well Operations Agent

Monitors well performance data continuously. Identifies failure patterns before they cause downtime. Flags anomalies in real-time to the right person.

Replaces: Daily production reporting, manual data review, reactive problem-solving

Regulatory

Compliance Agent

Auto-generates required regulatory reports (TX RRC, NM OCD, CAPP). Tracks submission deadlines. Flags upcoming inspections. Never misses a deadline.

Replaces: Manual report drafting, deadline tracking, compliance checkups

Business

Well Economics Agent

Calculates per-well ROI, breakeven prices, NPV scenarios. Updated daily as commodity prices and production data change. Delivered as a weekly dashboard.

Replaces: Spreadsheet modeling, manual economics updates, ad-hoc analysis requests

Slide 09

FAQ — Answering the hard questions

If someone's skeptical, here's what to say.

"We already have SAP / Pi System / Excel for this."
Those tools help humans do the work. We do the work for you. You keep using your existing systems — we just replace the human hours, not the infrastructure.
"AI makes mistakes. We can't trust it with compliance."
Correct — and that's why every report we generate gets reviewed by a human domain expert before submission. The AI does the drafting, the human does the approving. 90% of the work is done by AI, the human validates the 10% that matters.
"What if the AI goes down?"
Our AI runs on redundant cloud infrastructure. If something fails, a human on our team gets alerted immediately. We have SLAs for response time. An AI system that alerts humans when it needs help is better than a human who doesn't flag issues over the weekend.
"Our data is sensitive. How do we know it's secure?"
We don't store your production data long-term. The AI processes it to generate outputs, then the data stays with you. We're not a data aggregation play — we're a work-for-hire operation.
"This sounds expensive."
A mid-size O&G company spends $200-500k/year on manual operational labor for tasks our AI does in a fraction of the time. Our fee is a fraction of that cost, and we guarantee the work gets done.
"Can we cancel if it doesn't work?"
Yes. The pilot is a flat $5k with no long-term commitment. The full engagement is month-to-month after the pilot. If the ROI isn't there, you cancel.
