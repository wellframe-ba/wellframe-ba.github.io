Filled-In Example — Permian Basin Operator

How Permian Basin Operator Achieved 40% Reduction in Reporting Time

Production Operations — deployed in 2 weeks

The Challenge

A large independent operator in the Permian Basin was running daily production reports manually across 12 rigs. Two full-time analysts spent 6-8 hours each morning collecting well data from SCADA, normalizing it in Excel, and producing a PDF report for the operations team. Turnaround was 48 hours, which meant decisions were always based on yesterday's data. In addition, compliance monitoring for HSE metrics was entirely reactive — violations were caught in weekly audits, not in real time.

The Solution

Wellframe deployed three AI agents integrated with the operator's SCADA, SAP, and SharePoint systems. The Production Reporting agent autonomously collected well data every four hours, calculated key performance indicators, and generated a structured daily report — reducing turnaround from 48 hours to under 4. An HSE Monitoring agent was added in phase two to track compliance metrics across all 12 sites, flagging violations within 15 minutes of occurrence and routing alerts to the HSE manager via Teams.

What We Built

  • Data Integration Layer: Connected to SCADA (real-time well data), SAP (inventory and logistics), and SharePoint (document management) via secure REST APIs
  • Production Reporting Agent: Autonomously collects well data every 4 hours, calculates rates, tubing head pressure, water-cut, and 12 other KPIs, generates structured PDF reports delivered to operations team inbox and SharePoint
  • HSE Monitoring Agent: Tracks compliance metrics (flare monitoring, spill events, safety incident reports) across all 12 sites, flags violations within 15 minutes, routes to HSE manager via Microsoft Teams
  • Escalation & Alerting Workflow: Critical anomalies trigger immediate alerts to on-call engineers; non-critical items logged for weekly review
  • Operations Dashboard: Real-time visibility into all 12 rigs, agent performance metrics, and automated report history — accessible on desktop and mobile
