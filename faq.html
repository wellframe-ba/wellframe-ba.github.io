Frequently Asked Questions

For VP and Director level conversations

General

What exactly is an "AI agent" in this context?
Think of an AI agent as a highly specialized digital worker that understands Oil & Gas terminology, can access your data systems, and autonomously completes tasks — like drafting a daily production report, checking for compliance violations, or summarizing a seismic dataset — without requiring a human to initiate each step.
How is this different from a chatbot or automation script?
A chatbot responds to direct questions and has limited memory. An automation script follows a fixed set of rules. An AI agent reasons across complex, unstructured data, adapts to changing conditions, and can handle multi-step workflows with judgment — like deciding whether a pressure anomaly warrants an alert, a maintenance request, or escalation to an engineer.
Do we need to replace our existing systems?
No. Wellframe integrates with your existing data infrastructure — ERP, SCADA, LIMS, SharePoint, cloud storage, and industry-specific platforms. We connect to your systems; we don't replace them. Most integrations are complete within two weeks.
How long does a typical engagement last?
A pilot engagement runs 90 days. After that, clients typically move to an ongoing production contract. We structure engagements this way so you can validate the ROI before committing long-term.

Technical

What data systems can you integrate with?
We work with the major enterprise systems used in Oil & Gas: SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics, OSIsoft PI, Aspen Tech, Petrel, GeoFrame, and most SCADA platforms. We also connect to custom databases, SharePoint, and REST APIs. If your system has an API or a database, we can likely connect to it.
How do you handle proprietary Oil & Gas data?
Your data is your data. We never use it to train models for other clients. We offer air-gapped deployment options for clients with strict data sovereignty requirements. All data processing can occur within your network environment. We sign custom data handling agreements and are ISO 27001 certified.
What is your security and data privacy approach?
We operate on a least-privilege, zero-trust architecture. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't store Oil & Gas operational data long-term — agents process and respond in real time. We undergo annual third-party security audits and maintain SOC 2 Type II certification.
Can you work with our existing AI/ML teams?
Absolutely. Many of our enterprise clients have internal AI/ML capabilities. We position ourselves as a delivery and integration partner — we build the agents and operational workflows, and your team retains full visibility and control over the models, data, and decision logic. We can also integrate with models your team has already built.

Commercial

What is the typical investment range?
Pilots start at $15,000 per month for a 90-day engagement with 1-2 agents. Full production deployments typically run $45,000 per month with unlimited agents and dedicated support. Enterprise agreements with multi-site deployment and custom integrations are priced individually.
Do you offer fixed-price or time-and-materials?
Pilots are scoped and priced as fixed-fee engagements — you know exactly what you are getting and what it costs. Production and Enterprise agreements are typically subscription-based with a monthly fee, giving you predictability and us the incentive to deliver ongoing value.
What guarantees come with a pilot?
If you don't see measurable results within the 90-day pilot — defined by agreed-upon KPIs — we will extend the engagement at no additional cost or provide a partial refund. We are that confident in the ROI of well-scoped agent deployments.
Who owns the agents we build together?
You own the agents. Once deployed, the agents and their configurations are yours. We don't retain any rights to the agents built for your organization, and you are free to continue, expand, or modify them independently. We provide documentation, configuration access, and transition support if you ever want to bring the work in-house.

Timeline & Process

How fast can we see results?
Most clients see their first live agent results within two weeks of kickoff. Tangible operational improvements — measurable reductions in report turnaround time, data processing speed, or compliance violations — typically appear within 30-45 days. By the end of a 90-day pilot, you will have clear ROI data.
What does the first 90 days look like?
Days 1-14: integration and configuration. Days 15-30: first agents go live with monitoring. Days 31-60: expansion to additional use cases with your team providing feedback. Days 61-90: performance review, ROI documentation, and planning for production scale. Throughout, we provide weekly status updates and direct access to your dedicated team.
Do we need internal resources to manage the agents?
Minimal. We will train a designated point of contact on agent monitoring and basic configuration changes. Day-to-day management, performance tuning, and infrastructure maintenance are handled by Wellframe. You own the outcomes; we own the operations.
